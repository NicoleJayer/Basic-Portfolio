About Me

Hello there, my name is Nicole and this is my online portfolio. I am a recent graduate of James Madison University with a double major in Communications Studies and Media Arts & Design. After some time working for a non-profit in Washington, D.C. I decided my passion was not in communications, but in software development.

I enrolled in the GW Coding Bootcamp and began my journey into the world of software development in May 2018. I look forward to enhancing my skills as a developer and expanding on my professional career. Check out my porfolio for a collection of my in-class and personal projects.
